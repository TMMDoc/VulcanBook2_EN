Definition

Programming With the TC_LEAD_IN cycle call-up in the NC program, technology parameters for the optimum approach strategy are selected after a microjoint. The cycle is possible in the following machines: ■ TruLaser Series 3000 ■ TruLaser Series 5000 ■ TruLaser Series 7000 TC_LEAD_IN("Approach table", approach direction): = ■ Approach table: − The tables contain all relevant information about parameters and methods of the approach procedure. ■ Approach direction: − Depending on the parameter setting, the programmed contour is approached to the left or right of the direction of machining.

− TC_LEAD_IN ("Approach table", T_LEFT): Approaching the programmed contour on the left of the direction of movement.

− TC_LEAD_IN ("Approach table", T_RIGHT): Approaching the programmed contour on the right of the direction of movement.
