Before the start of processing the Laser technology for a contour, it is important to select the suitable parameters for the particular contour and update it. These parameters will be updated for every contours in the program. The functional parameters of Laser technology are as follows:

  • Piercing Method (PM)

  • Cutting Method (CM)

  • Ending Method (EM)

  • Z_Retract_Height (ZRH)

The parameter values are stored in the P-fields and for better understanding the P- fields are globally declared internally in the program as follows:

*#globdef* PM P1904

*#globdef* CM P1905

*#globdef* EM P1906

*#globdef* ZRH P1907

*# globdef* Update_Param G22 L9400 (will call %9400 sub program to update parameters)

Parameter Structure:

PM=20.000 CM=100.000

EM=1.000 ZRH=20

Piercing Method

The Piercing methods are defined based on the combination of different types of piercing available.

  1. The Piercing methods are defined as Floating numbers = 20.123

  2. The numbers before the decimal point represents the “Piercing ID”

  3. The numbers after the decimal point represents the “Piercing AID”

Cutting Methods:

  1. The Cutting methods are defined based on process & length of contours.

  2. The Cutting methods are represented as Floating numbers = 123.123

  3. The numbers before the decimal point represents the “Process Methods”

  4. The numbers after the decimal point represents the “Miscellaneous Control”

Process Methods:

1st digit: Process 2nd digit: Contour 3rd digit: Distance Regulation

0 = No process

0 = Large contour

0 – Height Control (HC) ON

1 = Cutting

1 = Medium contour

1 – HC Freeze in Lead-In & ON during Cutting

2 = *Special Cutting

2 = Small contour

2 – HC ON Positive direction & OFF Negative direction

3 = Marking

 — 

3 – Height Control (HC) OFF

4 = *Special marking

 — 

 — 

5 = Vaporization

 — 

 —

Ending Methods:

The Ending methods are defined based on the retract distance of Z-axis along with the Frog jump, HC freeze, etc., at the end.

  1. The Ending methods are defined as Floating numbers = 20.123

  2. The numbers before the decimal point represents the “End ID”

  3. The numbers after the decimal point represents the “End control”
