The remaining portion of a nested sheet can be cut and removed for easy handling of the jobs. In order to remove the remnant part, the cutting need to be started from outside the sheet and end beyond the sheet to make sure the part is completely removed from the jobs. For easy understanding, the remnant cut can be differentiated as two parts as follows:

  1. Start Remnant Cut

  2. End Remnant Cut

In a nesting job, there may be need for start of remnant cut (or) end of remnant cut (or) both depends on the availability of the remnant sheet.
