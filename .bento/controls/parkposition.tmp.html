By activating this program, it helps to move the X and Y axis to the respective park position as configured in technology parameters.

It is mainly used for service work (e.g., working on chain, proximity switch).

To move the pallet, Z axis should be in park position 115mm in KL59 cutting head. Not required to move X and Y axis to the park position.

Navigate to Configure → Machine settings → Tech parameters → Laser technology → Custom paramters1
