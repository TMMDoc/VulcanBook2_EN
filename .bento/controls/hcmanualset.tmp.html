Height control function is used to check the height between nozzle and workpiece manually.

By activating this program, it asks to enter Gain Factor and Nozzle Height. Here, the Gain factor denotes the speed of the movement and nozzle height denotes the height of the nozzle. It helps to check the height between the nozzle and the workpiece. User can also choose the required control mode.

Control mode

Control mode will control the process of distance regulation.

During the thin sheet piercing or other special conditions, selecting height control ON will improve the process time.

For certain conditions such as Scrap cut, Edge detection etc., there may be a need for the control of distance based on directions, freeze, or completely switch off.
