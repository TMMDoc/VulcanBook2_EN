By activating this program, user can confirm the point of indication where positioning diode captures. Work flow is to function the cut hole and check the nozzle centre is aligned correctly and confirm by saving it.

By enabling cut hole, it helps to cut the hole with the input of X and Y axis values.

By enabling check, it helps to confirm the position of nozzle centre with the cut hole. Both cut hole and nozzle centre should be aligned correctly.

By enabling save, it helps to save the offset value of confirmed position of cut hole aligned with nozzle centre.
