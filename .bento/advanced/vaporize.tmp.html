When processing foiled material it is often necessary to vaporize the foil first. This could be done with Vulcan directly on the machine.

To activate the vaporize sheet function, go to the edit page:

  • Select at Edit-→ Part -→ Setting-→ Vaporizing

Mode has four type:

  1. None;

  2. Pierce (only vaporizing the pierce points);

  3. Pierce & approach (Vaporizing Pierce & approach);

  4. Full laser path (Vaporizing Full laser path)
