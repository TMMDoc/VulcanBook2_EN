During piercing process, laser is monitoring the reflected power. If piercing is finished before the defined piercing time, laser control is able to stop the piercing and continue cutting directly. The advantage of PierceLine is - reduced sheet processing time - reduced wear of the slats

The parameters for the PierceLine sensor are done in the technology tables

The parameters listed in "Pierce sensors", how to use is:

  1. without piercing sensor: means not to active PierceLine function

  2. with plasma line : not available on the L99

    with PierceLine: this is used for controlled piercing, not the laser device is not available, no need now

  3. with PierceLine & ramp cycle: active the PierceLine function and piercing with ramp cycles and the max piercing time is lower than "setting piercing time" / "Ramp cycle time".
